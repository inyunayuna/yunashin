Another Audience

Design Research (in process)
Nov 2025 - Present
Individual Project

Overview

corner cast is an AR-based news platform for mobile or VR device invironment providing issues with read-aloud-experience to encourage users to spend more time focusing on not only mainstream articles but also news articles from every corner.

Background & Goal

Receiving serious issues around the world through journalism is surely an part of daily life continued since the early days of photojournalism. However, users who got used to quickly pay attention, enjoy briefly, then move on to the next page looking at their devices endlessly are now established in their daily lives, 'serious, complex, and important issues worldwide'. It is getting difficult to spend enough time carefully since services and providers are encouraging concentration behavior of ‘easy & quick UX’.

Project ‘corner cast’ is an alternative news platform that induces the experience of learning 'with time and effort' by reading these important issues aloud. Through reading-aloud experience that inevitably requires time, stamina, concentration and participatory behavior, users come to accept issues along with alternative simultaneous reading and cognition.
